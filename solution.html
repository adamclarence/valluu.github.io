Data workload lifecycle
Valluu has been created to be an end-to-end solution, covering the 5 stages of the data workload management lifecycle. Each stage is done within the solution, creating a single point of action and governance.
Currently, this lifecycle is distributed amongst multiple business and IT teams, creating a huge amount of process complexity and a failure of the business being able to respond to the required time-to-value window. The current complexity of the lifecycle, not only produces opportunity-loss, it increases operating costs and creates data governance risks, with industry average fines being in the region of $25-25M.

Applications in containers
Gartner predicts that by 2022, more than 75% of global organizations will be running Containerized Applications in Kubernetes for Production Services.
Whether you are still about to start your journey to the Cloud , are part-way-through or trying to work out how to optimize your new container based environment in Kubernetes, to reduce the time-to-value from Data exploitation, Valluu has addressed a key prohibitor, Data Workload Management.
Time-to-value
Valluu creates time-to-value by enabling the creation of required data workloads by the respective business user.
Valluu removes the need for or reliance on the IT Function, by giving the business user the ability to create new data workloads without the need to code. Scheduling is done automatically at the first available opportunity, through real-time monitoring of server capacity availability.
Reduced complexity
Valluu addresses areas that are creating major complexity and associated cost issues, namely IT infrastructure integration, IT engineering and data workload execution.
Valluu creates and manages all the required touchpoints of the lifecycle, through a single user-language native interface and provides a centralised procedure for budget management, full procedural audit, data compliance recording & reporting, and zero-trust access governance.
Operational savings
Valluu achieves operational savings, by reducing time, effort and resource costs in data workload development and lifecycle management.
Valluu allows us to put the use cases through the value estimator, and for a medium size enterprise, the estimated saving is $25M per annum in operational costs. In addition, data workload management, without Valluu, carries high data governance risk and recent fines have averaged between $20-$35M.
CO2 Savings
According to governmental studies (USA, Australia, UK), it is estimated that 82.5% of energy is lost from the power station to the requesting server.
Valluu directly reduces the amount of energy required by fully utilising physical & virtual servers by up to their operational processing limit of 80% (operating software consumes ~20% of processing availability), thereby reducing CO2 emissions. For a medium size enterprise, this is the equivalent of 50,000 Car Miles saved or 961 number of trees planted to offset CO2 emissions.
